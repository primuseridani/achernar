the future is now

Achernar is a small, indie development studio based in the Capital Region of Denmark. 🇩🇰


We develop open-source software, including video games and more productive software. We work with embedded systems as well as hosted ones.

vision

The goal of Achernar is to promote modern and robustly written open-source projects and alike.


In other words, our vision is to develop a human and clean industry and community for technology.


Currently, our roadmap is as follows:


  • Complete the webservice before autumn, 2024

  • Release our first game by 2024

  • Release early-access for Bowshock on Steam by 2025

  • Restructure to a normal sole proprietorship

team

As Achernar is currently registered as a PMV (lesser sole proprietorship), Gabriel Bjørnager Jensen is currently our only member.

inception

Achernar was founded in the summer of 2024 by I, Gabriel Bjørnager Jensen.


Our domain – achernar.dk – was already registered in the winter of 2021, at that time being used for hosting on-line source code repositories. This was, however, quickly outsourced to mandelbrot.dk after I had managed to secure that domain.


achernar.dk was then, in the mean time, used for hosting a few look-up references. This mostly served as my own notes for school – mainly mathematics, physics, and chemistry – but quickly grew unorganised and umaintained, although I did have plans to expand the lexicon.


The idea of a company had started in the spring of 2020. At that time it would've been named after the binary system Sheliak (Bayer: β Lyræ). But it was this idea that evolved into Achernar.
